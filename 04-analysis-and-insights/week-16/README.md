## This Week's Focus

Rewrite your notebook so every chart has a clear takeaway and a possible action.

**Topics:**

- Write chart interpretations in 2–3 sentences
- Separate observation, implication, recommendation
- Identify which KPIs matter most to the audience
- Turn findings into business or policy actions
- Avoid jargon and unsupported claims
- Create a narrative flow across charts
